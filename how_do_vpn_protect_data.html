How do VPNs protect user data

How do VPNs protect your browsing data

VPNs protect your browsing data by encrypting your internet connection and routing it through a secure server. This process masks your IP address, making it difficult for third parties to track your online activities.

Encryption

VPNs use encryption protocols to secure your data. This means that even if someone intercepts your internet traffic, they won't be able to read it without the decryption key.

No-logs

Most VPN companies have a no logging policy where they will not store any information about what your search history, online activity, or connection details. This means that your searching and your logs will not be able to be read by a hacker or a corporation.
